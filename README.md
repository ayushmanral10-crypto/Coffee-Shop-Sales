# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing coffee shop sales data using Microsoft Excel.
An interactive dashboard was created to track sales performance, identify trends, and generate actionable business insights.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales by day, hour, and month
Identify peak sales time
Compare performance across store locations
Understand customer purchasing behavior
Provide business recommendations

🛠️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Charts (Line, Bar, Pie)
Slicers
Dashboard Design

📊 Dashboard Features
Total Sales, Transactions, and Average Order Value KPIs
Sales trend by hour and month
Performance comparison across store locations
Top 5 products by sales
Product category distribution
Interactive filters using slicers

🔍 Key Insights
Total Sales: $698K+ from 149K+ transactions
Peak sales occur during 8 AM – 10 AM
Sales decline after 10 AM and evening hours
Coffee is the top-selling category (~39%)
Hell’s Kitchen is the best-performing location
Sales show steady growth from January to June

💡 Business Recommendations
Increase staffing during morning peak hours
Promote high-selling products (coffee-based items)
Improve performance of low-performing locations
Offer discounts during low-sales periods (evening)
